Howdy all!

As many of you Console folk have been letting us know, there is a lot of feedback about the Console Edition of the game and we have been listening! Our Development Team has been hard at work making sure we have as many of those issues covered as possible. While not everyone will see their issue covered in this update, we have a ton of good stuff for you today.

You may have seen the more precise and condensed list on our Social Media, but as always, we have the full Changelog for everyone to view and enjoy!

Some of the highlights of this particular Update are:
- Memory Leaks being addressed which cause random crashes
- Crown Authority Issues
- Inability to change Primary Title
- Corrupted Saves when auto-saving

So without further ado, here is that Changelog bringing fixes & improvements to Crusader Kings III on Console:

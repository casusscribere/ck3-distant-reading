Wow!  

What a year it has been! It is officially one year (and some change) since the game launched. We have had a ton of laughs, lots of stories, some tears were shed, and we have all learned so very much from each.  

We are so very pleased to have such a welcoming and helpful Community to help guide us and support us through all of our endeavors so far. Without each and every one of you and your terrific and insightful views on everything from the state of the game to your favorite memes, we would not have such a warm and happy place to call our own.  

We put together a little video with a few of our friends to celebrate and we hope you enjoy. While we did not get a chance to get everyone in on this video that we wanted to, we still feel it has a spot for everyone to enjoy!  

Here's to a year that was well spent and many more to make this Community and game and even more robust and better place for everyone! ![:)](data:image/gif;base64,R0lGODlhAQABAIAAAAAAAP///yH5BAEAAAAALAAAAAABAAEAAAIBRAA7 "Smile    :)")
